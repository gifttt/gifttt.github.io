About

I am a Principal Researcher at the Puey Ungphakorn Institute for Economic Research (PIER), Bank of Thailand. My research spans labor economics, public economics, and inflation expectations, with a growing interest in AI and machine learning applications in economics.

I hold a Ph.D. in Economics from Arizona State University, with additional graduate training in Statistics. Before joining PIER, I served as a policy analyst at Thailand's National Economic and Social Development Board.
